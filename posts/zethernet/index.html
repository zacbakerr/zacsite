About

Zethernet is a Ethereum blockchain explorer. One of the pillars of a blockchain is the ability for anyone to view someone else’s transactions, account balance, and more. Zethernet allows you to do this. You can search by Ethereum address, transaction hash, block number, and more. Feel free to check out my address (zacbaker.eth) or anyone else’s. It’s all in real time.

Next Steps
